# Electric Vehicle Population & Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of the Electric Vehicle (EV) market, tracking adoption trends, manufacturer dominance, and technological performance metrics. The goal of this project is to understand the growth trajectory of clean alternative energy vehicles and analyze key distribution factors.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Key Metrics (KPIs)
* *Total Vehicles*: 280K
* *Total Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV)*: 224K (80.02%)
* *Total Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV)*: 56K (19.98%)
* *Average Model Year*: 2022
* *Average Electric Range*: 39.17 miles

---

## 📈 Key Insights & Features Visualized
* *EV Adoption Trend*: Tracks the massive spike in EV registrations over the years, peaking significantly around 2023-2024.
* *Top Manufacturers: Highlights industry leaders dominated by **TESLA (0.11M vehicles)*, followed by Chevrolet, Nissan, Ford, and Kia.
* *Market Share (BEV vs. PHEV): A donut chart showing that Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) hold the majority share at **80.02%* compared to Plug-in Hybrids.
* *Top 10 Models by Average Electric Range*: A bar chart identifying top-performing models like the Roadster, I-Pace, Bolt EV, and Model S based on range capacity.
* *Geographical Distribution: A tree map displaying EV density across major cities, with **Seattle (43.25K)* leading the distribution, followed by Bellevue, Redmond, and Vancouver.
* *Interactive Filters: Dynamic slicers for *Model Year, Make, and Electric Vehicle Type for deeper data exploration.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
* *Microsoft Power BI*: Dashboard design and advanced data visualization.
* *Power Query*: Data cleaning, handling missing values, and transformation.
* *DAX*: Implemented custom measures for calculating vehicle totals, percentages, and performance averages.
